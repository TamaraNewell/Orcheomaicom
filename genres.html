THE GENRES OF DANCE

WHAT IS A DANCE GENRE?

A dance genre is a certain type of dance, or a dance style.

WHAT ARE SOME STYLES/ GENRES OF DANCE?

There are many styles of dance but, for the sake of simplicity they are catergorized into a few large groups which are: ballet, contemporary, hip-hop, latin, ballroom, and folk dance.There are sometimes where one dance style will be combined with another style, but other times dance genres will be divided into sub-categories. For example, styles like b-boying, popping, locking, and stepping are all sub-categories of the hip-hop dance genre.

BALLET

Ballet is one of the world's most popular dance genres. Ballet dancers are recognized for their grace, flexiblity, and athleticism. Ballet originated in the fifteenth century, during the italian renaissance, then later on was made into a theatre dance form in France and Russia.

CONTEMPERARY

Contemporary is a genre of dance that is a blend of various other styles and techniques such as classical ballet, jazz, modern dance, lyrical. This style was created during the mid-twentieth century and has become one of the most widely learned dance genres for trained dancers throughout the world, especially in the United States and Europe. Contemporary dancers venture to connect the body and mind through very expressive and fluid movements.

HIP-HOP

Hip Hop Dance is a style of movement characterized by bounces and rocks, executed to Hip Hop music. Hip-Hop dance came to be during the late 1960's and early 1970's, inspired by the movements of African dancing and incorporating characteristics of modern dance and tap.

LATIN

Latin dance is a dance style that is a fusion of dance styles from cultures around the world. Indigenous American, Iderian, and West African cultures influence this dance style. Latin dance dates all the way back to the 15th century, but gained popularity in the early 1930's Latin dancers are known for the sensual steps and strong bonds between partners.

FOLK DANCE

A folk dance is a dance mirrors the life, culture, and tradition of the people of that live in a certain region or country. Folk dances are usually performed at social events like festivals and dance gathering, by people who most of the time have little to no professional training. Folk dance is a part of all cultures around the world and has dates back several centuries.
